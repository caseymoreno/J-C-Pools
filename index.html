ADVANTAGES OF FIBERGLASS

Why Fiberglass?

Maintenance

Say goodbye to constant maintenance and repair. The gel layer of our fiberglass pools is smooth, non-porous, and does not alter the chemical balance of the pool. Fiberglass pools don't need to be acid washed, any algae built up can be easily swept off with a brush.

Costs

Don't break the bank! Fiberglass pools are the perfect addition to your home, and when compared to concrete pools, fiberglass offers all the same great benefits with a fraction of the cost.

Fast Installation

Swim Sooner! Our Pools Shells are manufactured in Tampa and with easy installation, you can be swimming within weeks!
